About IEG Plastics

IEG Plastics, LLC is headquartered in Bellefontaine, OH. We are an ISO9001:2015 certified plastic parts manufacturer servicing the automotive, sporting goods, and consumer goods industries. Our production facilities are setup to handle small runs of a few hundred parts up to large production runs of over a million parts. We also design, build, and service plastic injection molds.

Mission Statement

Our Mission is to be a "best-in-class" producer of custom injection molded products and assemblies; creating value for our customers, associates, and community.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to provide creative, quality focused, cost effective solutions while continually improving our company, products, and services. This will be accomplished through teamwork and the utilization of our most important asset, our people.

Quality

We are committed to being "best-in-class" in the industry so we can pass that value and expertise along to our customers. The company has invested in rigorous industry standard training and certifications to keep current with the latest technological advances and best quality assurance practices.

Equipment

IEG offers state of the art precision equipment which allows us to provide you with the best quality product. The company's ability to tackle complex projects is what makes IEG a leader in the mold design and mold making industry
